Your Guide to Voting

One of the most important things you can do is to vote in elections. Voting is a fundamental right and responsibility in a democratic society.

Select an Active Election

To register to vote on Star Voting Company, you must first create a voter account. Registration requires basic personal information to verify your identity and ensure that each voter is eligible to participate in elections. This process helps maintain fairness and transparency across the platform. During registration, you will be asked to provide accurate details such as your name and contact information. Make sure all information entered is correct, as incorrect details may delay or prevent successful registration. Once your account is created, you will gain access to your personal voter dashboard. This dashboard allows you to manage your profile and track elections you are eligible to participate in

Create Your Voter Account

On your dashboard, you will see a list of elections currently open for voting. Each election includes details such as the candidates, election rules, and the voting deadline. Take your time to review this information carefully before proceeding.

Click on the election you wish to participate in to view the candidates. You can read candidate profiles, review their manifestos, and understand what each candidate stands for. This ensures you make an informed decision.

When you are ready, proceed to the voting page. The platform will guide you step by step so you don't miss any important instructions before casting your vote.
